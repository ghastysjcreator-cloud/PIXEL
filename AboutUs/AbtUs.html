⭐ About PIXEL


Welcome to PIXEL! >:3

PIXEL is a website where people can create pixel art using a simple and easy-to-use editor. Whether you want to draw game sprites, landscapes, icons, random doodles, or just experiment with colors, PIXEL gives you a place to turn your ideas into artwork one pixel at a time.


👋 Who Are We?


Right now, PIXEL is a small project created and developed by a young programmer who enjoys learning new things and creating fun projects. >:3

The website started when I was around 10–11 years old after my online programming teacher suggested the idea as a project assignment. At first, I thought it would just be another programming task, but after I started working on it, I realized I actually enjoyed building it.

As time went on, the project grew bigger and bigger. New ideas appeared, new features were added, and what started as a simple assignment slowly became a real website that people can use.

While working on PIXEL, I learned a lot about programming, web design, problem-solving, and creativity. Some parts were easy, while others took a lot of patience. T_T

Sometimes a bug would take hours to find, and when I finally fixed it, it felt like hitting the jackpot. ⁷⁷⁷_⁷⁷⁷

🎨 What Is The Style Or Idea Behind PIXEL?


The main idea behind PIXEL is simplicity.

Many art programs can feel complicated when you first open them. There are lots of buttons, menus, tools, and settings that can be confusing.

PIXEL was designed to be different.

The goal was to create a place where someone could open the editor and start drawing almost immediately.

Pixel art itself is simple in appearance but incredibly creative. Tiny squares can become characters, worlds, buildings, animals, objects, logos, and entire stories.

That idea inspired the design of the website.

The green colors, simple layout, and easy-to-understand tools are all meant to create a friendly and welcoming experience. :3

Instead of focusing on complexity, PIXEL focuses on creativity.

The website is designed to let the artwork be the star of the show. >:3
